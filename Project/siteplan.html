CookWell – Recipe & Meal Planning App

Tagline: Healthy, simple cooking made personal.

Site Name

CookWell was chosen because it’s memorable, descriptive, and promotes a sense of health and simplicity in home cooking. It emphasizes cooking "well" – both in quality and health-consciousness.

Optional domain: cookwellplanner.com (availability not required)

Site Purpose

The purpose of CookWell is to serve as a personal digital cookbook and weekly meal planner. Users can browse healthy and simple recipes, filter them by calorie count or prep time, and create a weekly meal plan using a simple interactive form. The site promotes better eating habits and helps users stay organized with their meals.

Scenarios

  • What can I cook this week that fits my 30-minute weeknight schedule?
  • Are there any low-calorie recipes I can use for lunch prep?

Color Schema

  • Main Accent Color: #2e8b57 (used for headings, buttons, and highlights)
  • Secondary Accent Color: #f4a261 (used for icons, section dividers, and UI elements like sliders)
  • Background: White (#ffffff)
  • Text Color: Dark Gray (#333333)

Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display – for elegant, readable headings
  • Body Text: Roboto – for modern, clean readability
